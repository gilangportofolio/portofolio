University Widyatama
2019 - 2023

Bachelor of Informatics Engineering

During my studies, I was actively involved in various campus organizations and academic activities. Below are some of the experiences I gained during my university years:
  • Leader of the ERP Center Website

    I led a team of 5 people to develop the ERP Center website using the Waterfall method. The project aimed to improve the efficiency of certificate collection and book purchasing at the ERP Center, and it lasted for 4 months.

  • Program Representative at the Thematic KKN LLDIKTI Region IV

    I represented the Study Program in the Thematic KKN LLDIKTI Region IV with the theme "Higher Education Moving Together, Synergizing, and Collaborating to Build Villages," which took place from June to August 2022. This activity involved 17 participants from 7 campuses and various departments. The programs we implemented included regional product promotion, stunting prevention, teaching, information systems, and creating film documentation.

  • Leader of the Application Development (Petani Sejahtera and SIDUDA)

    I led a team of 7 people in developing an application product using the SDLC methodology with an Agile Scrum approach. We successfully created several platforms, including a website for visitors, an admin website for monitoring, and Android/iOS applications for farmers and couriers. This project lasted 5 months, and the beta version of the app has been released on the Play Store and App Store. I also managed different perspectives from stakeholders, farmers, admins, and academics to create a product that met the needs of all parties.

  • Member of the PKM (Community Service) Program

    I participated in the PKM program, focusing on the socialization of village administration management through digital archiving in Karyawangi Village.

  • Member of the Galeri Investasi Widyatama Creative Team

    As a creative member, I was responsible for creating digital materials, including educational posters and certificates, for activities in the Widyatama Investment Gallery.

  • Member of the Galeri Investasi Widyatama Education Team

    As a member of the education team, my role was to deliver educational materials on stocks and investments to the members of the Widyatama Investment Gallery. The materials were approved by the team leader before being presented to all gallery members.
